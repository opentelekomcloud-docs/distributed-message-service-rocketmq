How Many Topics Are Supported?
==============================

As shown in :ref:`Table 1 <hrm-faq-005__table2197416103017>` and :ref:`Table 2 <hrm-faq-005__table52561620162313>`, the number of topics supported varies depending on the instance flavor.

.. _hrm-faq-005__table2197416103017:

.. table:: **Table 1** Instance flavors (DMS for RocketMQ 5.x basic edition single-node)

   +---------------------+--------------------+-----+-------------+----------------------+
   | Flavor              | Storage Space (GB) | TPS | Max. Topics | Max. Consumer Groups |
   +=====================+====================+=====+=============+======================+
   | rocketmq.b1.large.1 | 100-30,000         | 500 | 50          | 500                  |
   +---------------------+--------------------+-----+-------------+----------------------+
